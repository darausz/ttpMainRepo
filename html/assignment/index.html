Recipe List

Recipe 1: Ham Egg Cheese in a Croissant

  • Ingredients:
    • 2 croissants
    • 2 slices of any cheese
    • 1-2 eggs
    • 2-4 slices of ham
  • Instructions:
    • cut both croissants in half horizontally and toast with cheese slices on top
    • pan fry ham until golden brown
    • put croissants onto a plate with ham on the one with no cheese
    • pan fry egg with salt and pepper and put it on the ham
    • optionally add mayo and serve by putting the croissants together like a sandwich

Recipe 2: Peanut Noodles

Ingredients Instructions
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tsp water
  • 8 oz noodles
  1. cook noodles in boiling water to desired softness and drain
  2. mix all of the ingredients into a bowl
  3. microwave until peanute butter is melted and mix well
  4. dish noodles and pour the sauce over
  5. mix well and optionally garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds
